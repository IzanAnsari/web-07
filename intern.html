Internship Experiences

During my internship at COMPSOFT TECHNOLOGIES Bangalore, I had the privilege of being part of a dynamic 4-member team that successfully developed the static website
"LEAF NOW." As the team leader, I gained valuable experience in collaborating effectively and learned how to function seamlessly within a team. The website

"LEAF NOW" is an e-commerce platform dedicated to promoting environmental awareness.

Users can easily register and log in to the website, where they can engage in buying and selling various plants. Through this project, I honed my skills in web development

and gained a deeper understanding of designing for a cause that aligns with environmental consciousness. I am excited to add this project to my portfolio as it exemplifies

my ability to work collaboratively, lead a team, and contribute to a meaningful cause through technology.
